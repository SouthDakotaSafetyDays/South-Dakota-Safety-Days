Volunteer With Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers at South Dakota Safety Days making communities safer. Whether you can help at events, assist with educational outreach, or support administrative tasks, your time makes a real difference. If you have specific questions, please email us at southdakotasafetydays@gmail.com

Thank you for submitting the form! We will reach out to you with details.

Time Commitment

  • Flexible opportunities from one-time events to ongoing roles
  • Most events require 3-6 hours of time
  • Choose your own schedule and availability

Volunteer Benefits

  • Make a meaningful impact in your community
  • Certificate of service for volunteer hours
  • Connect with fellow safety advocates

Requirements

  • Must be 18 years or older (16+ with parental consent)
  • Attend brief orientation session
  • Commitment to promoting safety in our communities
